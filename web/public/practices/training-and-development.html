PHR Guidelines / Work Instructions

TDG-1 — Establish Training Needs Assessment

  1. Conduct surveys and interviews to identify training gaps.
  2. Analyze project needs to prioritise training topics.
  3. Create a targeted training plan.

TDG-2 — Develop Comprehensive Training Curriculum [ISO 4.11.1]

  1. Design modules covering practical and theoretical skills.
  2. Include hands-on exercises and real-world projects.
  3. Define learning paths and prerequisites.

TDG-3 — Engage Subject Matter Experts (SMEs)

  1. Bring SMEs into training sessions and content review.
  2. Use mentorship to reinforce learning.

TDG-4 — Implement Blended Learning Approach

  1. Mix instructor-led sessions with online self-paced courses.
  2. Use workshops and case studies for practical application.

TDG-5 — Establish Mentorship Programs

  1. Pair junior and senior members for ongoing mentorship.
  2. Define mentorship goals and evaluation criteria.

TDG-6 — Allocate Dedicated Time for Learning

  1. Reserve time each week for training and study.
  2. Provide access to learning platforms and resources.

TDG-7 — Measure Training Effectiveness

  1. Run pre/post assessments and participant surveys.
  2. Track KPI improvements after training.
  3. Adjust the curriculum based on feedback and results.
