Empowered Teams

Promote collaboration, autonomy, accountability and continuous learning within cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality software.

Key aspects

  • Cross-functional teams: Teams include developers, testers, designers and product owners to enable collaboration and shared understanding.
  • Agile methodologies: Adopt Scrum or Kanban to enable iterative delivery and frequent feedback.
  • Self-organization: Give teams authority to make technical decisions and set priorities.
  • Continuous learning: Encourage training, knowledge sharing and experimentation.
  • Collaborative decision-making: Include team members in decisions for buy-in and ownership.
  • CI/CD practices: Promote frequent integration and delivery with automated testing and pipelines.
  • Transparent communication: Maintain open channels for progress and blockers.
  • Retrospectives & feedback: Conduct regular retros to identify improvements and act on feedback.

PHR Guidelines / Work Instructions

EMTG-1 — Establish cross-functional teams

  1. Form teams with varied skills and define roles.
  2. Encourage pairing and knowledge sharing.
  3. Rotate responsibilities to spread domain knowledge.

EMTG-2 — Promote autonomy and decision-making

  1. Delegate decision authority to teams with clear guardrails.
  2. Provide coaching and escalation paths when needed.
  3. Ensure teams have access to required resources.

EMTG-3 — Implement Agile methodologies

  1. Adopt Scrum or Kanban and follow ceremonies that support collaboration.
  2. Prioritize customer collaboration and iterative delivery.
  3. Continuously refine backlog and priorities.

EMTG-4 — Foster continuous learning

  1. Allocate time for training and skill development.
  2. Host internal knowledge sharing sessions.
  3. Encourage safe experimentation and postmortem learning.

EMTG-5 — Promote transparent communication

  1. Hold regular stand-ups and create shared communication channels.
  2. Share project updates and blockers openly.
  3. Practice active listening and constructive feedback.

EMTG-6 — Encourage collaborative decision-making

  1. Use collaborative sessions and voting where appropriate.
  2. Document decisions and rationale.
  3. Ensure decisions are revisited if conditions change.

EMTG-7 — Implement CI/CD practices

  1. Automate build, test and deployment steps.
  2. Use feature flags and trunk-based development where possible.
  3. Ensure a reliable pipeline for frequent releases.

EMTG-8 — Conduct regular retrospectives

  1. Hold retros after each iteration to capture improvements.
  2. Identify actionable items and assign owners.
  3. Review progress on action items regularly.

EMTG-9 — Encourage feedback loops

  1. Engage stakeholders and customers for feedback regularly.
  2. Use feedback to prioritise improvements and features.
  3. Measure outcomes and adjust based on results.

EMTG-10 — Nurture a positive team environment

  1. Recognise achievements and promote psychological safety.
  2. Provide support to resolve conflicts quickly.
  3. Invest in team wellbeing and job satisfaction.

EMTG-11 — Use licensed software [ISO 27001 A.18.1.2]

  1. Track required licenses and audit usage regularly.
  2. Educate teams on license compliance and risks.
  3. Replace unlicensed tools with approved alternatives.

EMTG-12 — Maintain a decision log [ISO 4.3.1]

  1. Create a centralized repository for decision records.
  2. Log context, options considered, and final choices.
  3. Reference decision IDs in related work items and commits.
